OpenFlights: Privacy policy

This document outlines OpenFlights' respect for your personal privacy as a user of the service.

  • Your flights will not be visible to other users, business partners or the public at large unless you opt to make them public.
  • Your password or e-mail address will not be shared with other users, business partners or the public at large.
  • OpenFlights may choose to display or aggregate your flights in anonymized format.
  • OpenFlights will comply with court orders to turn over your private information.
